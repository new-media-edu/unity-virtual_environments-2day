# Session 02: Unity Workshop - Day 2

Date: Thursday, April 23
Time: 10:00 am - 1:00 pm CET

## Session Focus

Session 02 builds on Day 1 by importing photogrammetry captures, shaping atmosphere through lighting, and adding simple interactivity through pre-made scripts and AI-assisted scripting.

## Prerequisites

- Unity 6 or the current version installed.
- ProBuilder installed.
- At least one .obj or .glb scan exported from the overnight assignment.

## Agenda

- Importing Reality
  - Bring overnight photogrammetry meshes into Unity.
  - Resolve scale and orientation issues.
  - Apply basic shaders and materials to captured textures.
- Atmosphere and Lighting
  - Adjust Skyboxes and Directional Lights.
  - Add Point Lights to simulate gallery spotlights.
  - Refine mood and readability of exhibition spaces.
- No-Code Interactivity
  - Introduce pre-made Trigger scripts, such as entering a zone to play a video.
  - Demonstrate AI-assisted generation of simple C# scripts for specific artistic behavior.
  - Example prompts include rotating an object slowly or changing color on click.
- Assisted Studio Time
  - Final hour for project refinement.
  - Individual support for technical and aesthetic decisions.

## Session Checklist

- Imported at least one scanned object from Polycam.
- Corrected scale and texture presentation.
- Added and tuned at least one lighting setup.
- Implemented one simple interactive behavior.
- Exported or saved a clean scene state for future iteration.

## Technical Notes

- ProBuilder location: Window > Package Manager > Unity Registry.
- Polycam: iOS and Android; free tier is sufficient for this workshop.
- Asset prep reminder: bring JPG/PNG images and MP4 videos for display in the virtual gallery.
